Our Mission

Built to fix what's been broken.

Email is the most important communication tool in the world — and it's been neglected for decades. Cluttered inboxes, aggressive ad tracking, weak privacy, zero intelligence. We built ThePost.Space because we believe your inbox should work for you, not against you.

We combine AI-powered sorting, end-to-end encryption, and thoughtful design into a single platform — built for professionals, teams, and anyone who's tired of the email chaos.

"We didn't set out to compete with Gmail. We set out to reimagine email for a world that expects privacy, intelligence, and simplicity as defaults — not add-ons."

From a distributed infrastructure spanning global regions, we're building the next generation of email — fast, private, and available to everyone, everywhere.

Private by design

End-to-end encryption on every message. We never read your emails, never sell your data, never serve you ads. Your inbox belongs to you.

AI-powered, not AI-dependent

Smart sorting, smart compose, smart replies — all running on-device where possible. Productivity that actually respects your privacy.

Zero compromise on speed

Messages delivered in under 50ms globally. Our infrastructure is engineered to eliminate delays at every layer, from send to inbox.

What Drives Us

The principles behind everything we build

From our encryption architecture to our support team — these values shape every decision we make.

Privacy First

End-to-end encryption isn't a premium feature — it's the default. We don't read your email, we don't profile you, and we will never monetise your inbox with ads.

Instant Delivery

We obsess over latency. Messages reach inboxes in under 50ms globally. Our distributed infrastructure is engineered to eliminate delays at every layer.

Intelligent by Default

AI that actually helps — smart sorting, spam elimination, compose assistance, and priority detection. All running with privacy-preserving models.

Zero Ads

Your inbox is not an advertising channel. We are subscription-funded, which means our incentives are perfectly aligned with yours: give you a great product.

Team-Ready

Shared inboxes, admin dashboards, SSO/SAML, and granular permissions — built for organisations of every size, without IT complexity.

Open Standards

We support IMAP, SMTP, and OpenPGP — you're never locked in. Export your data, use your favourite client, and stay in control of your email forever.

Always Improving

We ship, listen, and iterate. Feedback from every user conversation shapes our roadmap. There's no final version — only a better next one. Our changelog speaks for itself.
