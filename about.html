Our Story

Born from a real problem

International shipping is broken. It's slow, opaque, unpredictable — and nobody has fixed it. We built ThePost.Space because we believe you deserve to know exactly where your mail is, every step of the way.

Our founding team brings together logistics experts, software engineers, and design thinkers who share one obsession: making the movement of physical things as smooth and trackable as sending an email.

"We didn't set out to compete with postal services. We set out to reinvent them for a world that expects Amazon-speed and Apple-simplicity."

From our distributed network of processing hubs across 150+ countries, we're building the infrastructure for the next generation of international mail.

Our Values

What drives us every day

These principles guide everything we build — from our routing algorithms to our customer support.

Speed First

Every delay costs trust. We obsess over transit times, cutting unnecessary handoffs and routing inefficiencies out of the system.

Secure by Design

Your shipment data is yours. We use end-to-end encryption for tracking data and never share your information with third parties.

Truly Global

We build for every country equally. No second-class destinations. 150+ countries with the same quality of service and tracking.

Full Transparency

Know where your mail is at every moment. Real-time tracking, proactive notifications, and honest ETAs — no guessing games.

Human Support

Real people, not bots. Every support request reaches a trained specialist who knows our network and can actually solve your problem.

Sustainable Shipping

We carbon-offset every shipment by default and partner exclusively with carriers committed to reducing their environmental impact.
