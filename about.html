Our Mission

Built to fix what's been broken.

International shipping is broken. It's slow, opaque, and unpredictable — and nobody has truly fixed it. We built ThePost.Space because we believe you deserve to know exactly where your mail is, every step of the way.

We unite logistics expertise, software engineering, and product thinking around a single obsession: making the movement of physical things as seamless and trackable as sending an email.

"We didn't set out to compete with postal services. We set out to reinvent them for a world that expects Amazon-speed and Apple-simplicity."

From a distributed network spanning 150+ countries, we're building the infrastructure for the next generation of international mail — available to everyone, everywhere.

Global by design

Every route, every hub, every integration — engineered to work seamlessly across borders without compromise.

Privacy & security first

Your shipment data belongs to you. End-to-end encryption and strict data governance — no exceptions.

Radical transparency

Real-time tracking, honest ETAs, and proactive updates at every stage. No black boxes, ever.

What Drives Us

The principles behind everything we build

From our routing algorithms to our customer support — these values guide every decision we make.

Speed First

Every delay costs trust. We obsess over transit times, cutting unnecessary handoffs and routing inefficiencies out of the system.

Secure by Design

Your shipment data is yours. We use end-to-end encryption for tracking data and never share your information with third parties.

Truly Global

We build for every country equally. No second-class destinations — 150+ countries with the same quality of service and tracking.

Full Transparency

Know where your mail is at every moment. Real-time tracking, proactive notifications, and honest ETAs — no guessing games.

Human Support

Real people, not bots. Every support request reaches a trained specialist who knows our network and can actually solve your problem.

Sustainable Shipping

We carbon-offset every shipment by default and partner exclusively with carriers committed to reducing their environmental impact.

Our Approach

How we think and build

The philosophy behind our product decisions and the way we operate as a company.

Customer-Obsessed

Every feature, every update, every decision starts with one question: does this make life easier for the people sending and receiving mail? If not, we don't ship it.

Built to Scale

Our infrastructure is designed from day one to handle millions of parcels without degradation. Reliability isn't a feature — it's the foundation.

Privacy by Default

We collect only what's necessary, store it securely, and never monetise your data. Privacy isn't a policy document — it's baked into how we build.

Always Improving

We ship, listen, and iterate continuously. Feedback from every user interaction shapes our roadmap. There's no final version — only a better next one.
