❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

Can I switch plans at any time?+
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
What happens when I exceed my storage limit?+
We'll notify you when you reach 80% and 95% of your storage limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional storage. We won't delete your emails, but you won't be able to receive new ones until you free up space or upgrade.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?+
Absolutely. All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?+
Yes! We offer 50% off all paid plans for verified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with your organization details to apply for the discount.
How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?+
If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of any paid subscription, simply contact us for a full refund. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through. We want you to be completely happy with your choice.
Can I migrate my existing emails?+
Yes, we offer one-click migration from Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and any IMAP-compatible provider. Our migration tool preserves your folder structure, labels, and attachments. Business and Enterprise plans also include dedicated migration support.
