FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers. Can't find what you need? Use the form above.

What countries do you ship to?+
ThePost.Space covers 150+ countries worldwide. We're continuously expanding our network. During early access, we'll confirm coverage for your specific route before you commit.
How fast is your express delivery?+
Express international delivery is typically under 48 hours for major routes (Europe to North America, intra-EU, etc.). Standard delivery is 3–7 business days depending on the destination.
How does tracking work?+
Every shipment gets real-time tracking with GPS-level updates at each hub. You'll receive proactive notifications via email and SMS at every milestone — no more guessing games.
What items can I send?+
Letters, parcels, and documents up to 30kg. We comply with all international shipping regulations and provide guidance on prohibited items for each destination country.
How do I request early access?+
Use the contact form above or email hello@thepost.space. Early access users get priority onboarding, a dedicated account manager, and a special founding member rate locked in for life.
Is my shipment data secure?+
Yes. All shipment data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're GDPR compliant and our infrastructure runs on EU-based servers by default. We never sell your data.
