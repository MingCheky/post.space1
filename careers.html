Our culture

How we work

We're opinionated about how great products get built. Here are the principles that guide everything we do.

Ship Fast, Iterate

We prefer a good product today over a perfect one next month. Real user feedback drives everything.

Radical Transparency

Strategy, financials, roadmap — shared with everyone. No information siloes, no political games.

Deep Ownership

You own your domain end-to-end. No micromanagement, no waiting for approval. Ship and learn.

Written Culture

We default to async writing. Good writing forces clear thinking — and respects everyone's time.

Kindness First

We disagree openly but always with care and curiosity. No egos, no drama — just good people solving hard problems.

Raise the Bar

Every person we hire makes the team better. We're building something special — we need special people.
