Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand usage patterns, and deliver a more consistent experience. Technologies like local storage and session tokens serve similar purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for essential functionality, security, performance analytics, and experience improvements. We do not sell your data or use tracking cookies for third-party advertising.

ThePost.Space protects your shipment and account data end-to-end. No cookies contain tracking identifiers, delivery contents, or payment credentials.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential

Session tokens, CSRF protection, and infrastructure routing. Required for the service to function.

Required

Performance & Analytics

Anonymised load times, error rates, and feature adoption — processed in aggregate, never individually.

Optional

Preferences

Language selection, theme choice, notification settings, and layout customisations.

Optional

Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core features. Performance and preference cookies can be managed through your browser or our in-app privacy controls.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We minimise third-party cookies to the absolute essentials. Payment processors (Stripe) and support tools may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not embed advertising tracking pixels or data-broker scripts.

5. Managing Cookies

You have full control. Most browsers let you:

  • View and delete existing cookies at any time
  • Block third-party cookies by default
  • Automatically clear cookies when you close the browser
  • Set per-site cookie permissions

Disabling essential cookies will affect login, security, and core functionality. You can also adjust preferences through our cookie consent banner.

6. Do Not Track (DNT)

We honour DNT browser signals wherever technically feasible. Our analytics infrastructure is privacy-by-design and does not fingerprint or track users across sites, making DNT effectively redundant on ThePost.Space anyway.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy whenever our cookie practices change. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent website notice at least 14 days before taking effect.

8. Contact

Questions about our cookie practices? Reach our Privacy Team at privacy@thepost.space
