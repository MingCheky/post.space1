Security

Your privacy is non-negotiable

We built ThePost.Space with a security-first mindset. Your data belongs to you — always and without exception.

End-to-End Encryption

Every message is encrypted before it leaves your device. We physically cannot read your mail — by design.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We never store your encryption keys. Even in the unlikely event of a breach, your data remains completely unreadable.

SOC 2 Type II Certified

Independently audited and certified. We uphold the highest standards of data security and operational privacy.

GDPR & CCPA Compliant

Full compliance with global privacy regulations. Your data never leaves the EU without your explicit consent.
